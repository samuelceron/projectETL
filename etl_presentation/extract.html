Extraction process

Extract is the process of reading data from a database. In this stage, the data is collected, often from multiple and different types of sources.

In our case, let's say that each of the six tables come from different Steam company departments.


During the extract process, the team analyzed 409 columns and decided to use 18, having in mind several criteria, particularly:

  • Is the column useful to describe the video game?
  • Is the column a relevant variable to make a basic data analysis?
